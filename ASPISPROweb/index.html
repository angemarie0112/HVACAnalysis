Public procurement is a crucial process for all types of organizations, both public and private. While only large entities often invest in procurement systems, we hope to make this accessible for small and medium enterprises as well.

Procurement is a very important function within organizations as it supplies products and services to fuel operations. Making sure that procurement is properly managed assures the best results for every department, as all operations depend on its effectiveness.

Most experienced professionals will tell you how procurement is challenging.

We have heard the same general challenges from many of our clients:

  • Project failure due to ineffective procurement
  • Processes take too long and delay operations
  • Skills gap, often leading to poor results
  • Poor design and specifications result in poor project delivery
  • Procurement causes too much financial loss, they receive inflated bids or experience fraud

These challenges have contributed to making procurement the most vulnerable function within any entities, where we see between 5% and 30% of financial loss due to the disturbed procurement systems.
